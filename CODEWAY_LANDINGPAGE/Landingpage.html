    ECE

    Electronics and communication engineering (ECE) comprises communication engineering and the core of electrical engineering. A career in this field can be very diverse and rewarding.

    CSE

    Computer science is the study of computation, information, and automation.Computer science spans theoretical disciplines (such as algorithms, theory of computation, and information theory) to applied disciplines (including the design and implementation of hardware and software).Though more often considered an academic discipline, computer science is closely related to computer programming.

    MECH

    Mechanical engineering is the study of physical machines that may involve force and movement. It is an engineering branch that combines engineering physics and mathematics principles with materials science, to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems.It is one of the oldest and broadest of the engineering branches.
